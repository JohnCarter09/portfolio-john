Hey there, I'm John

About me:

Originally from Arizona, I moved to Colorado in 2009. I was stationed in Colorado Springs at Fort Carson where I was enlisted in the United States Army. Honorably discharged in 2012, I enrolled in an International Business Program at The University Of Colorado where I successfully finished my bachelor's degree in 2 & 1/2 years.

It was in college when I took my first human-computer interaction and design centered courses. College is when I really found my love for user centered design.


Fast forward a few years:

Currently, I work for an enterprise software company called RMS (Risk Managment Solutions) where I wear multiple hats in the UX realm. My work focuses primarily on all RMS (one) Platform product design initiatives including site-maps, high (and Low) fidelity wireframes, UI prototyping & responsive front-end development. Currently at RMS, we use React as our UI framework. A couple of my proudest projects to date, would be building out the P2 Design System at my previous company, and coding this portfolio website :) In the past, I have worked in very diverse environments. Everything from larger agencies, to small, nimble start-ups. I am passionate about creating effective user experiences that not only inspire, but educate and perform. In my free time I love photography, and writing about UX. Some of my recent articles can be seen on the UX Collective.
