Denver Beer Co.

I was contracted out by a local brewery, Denver Beer Co. to film a story of their Sun Drenched Music Festival last summer. The idea was to give the audiance a first person prospective of what it would be like to attend the festival. I enjoyed working with Denver Beer co. The team was very friendly and the patrons of the show were all so nice. I was able to include some drone videography into the video as well.

Boulder.

I Randomly get the urge to just go fly my drone from time to time. This was a summer evening last year up in Boulder, Colorado. I went up to the Flat Irons and found a trail where there was not a lot of people. I knew that some people would not take to kindly to the noise of the drone, especially in nature. Here is a quick video I put together of that quick trip to Boulder.

redRocks.

There is just something mystical about RedRocks after a rain storm, the rocks are darker than usual and there is always a low-hanging fog thats creates a interesting mystique. I woke up early one moring after a light rain in the city, and I decided to head up to Morrison for an early moring flight. Im pretty sure this is a no fly zone, but I went for it anyway and Im glad I did. This place at 7:00am is unequivocally beautiful. I sent the drone up and was able to capture some really amazing footage, I hope you enjoy.
